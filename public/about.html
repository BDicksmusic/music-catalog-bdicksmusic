My Musical Journey

From student to educator, my path has been shaped by incredible mentors, diverse experiences, and an unwavering passion for music.

Present
Multiple Roles & Positions
Currently serving as Adjunct Professor of Trumpet at Northern Arizona University, Co-Director of the Trumpeter's Multi-Track Competition and Seminar, and Program Director of Rhode Island Recording Ensemble. Also managing BDicksmusic YouTube channel with over 1K subscribers and 100K+ views.
Doctorate
Arizona State University
Obtained Doctorate in Trumpet Performance while serving as Teaching Assistant to renowned trumpeter Joe Burgstaller. This experience deepened my understanding of advanced performance techniques and pedagogy.
Master's Degree
University of Hartford
Earned Master's Degree in Trumpet Performance and Music Composition, studying trumpet with Phil Snedecor and composition with Robert Carl, David Macbride, and Gilda Lyons. This dual focus shaped my unique perspective as both performer and composer.
Bachelor's Degree
Columbus State University
Completed Bachelor of Arts degree in Music Education, studying with Robert Murray. This foundation in education has been instrumental in my teaching philosophy and approach.
